Where are you?

Hit the plus to add a new location, hit the minus to remove it, and drag and drop them to arrange them into your preferred order. The text you enter auto-saves when it looks like you're done entering it; to make it clear when you are, use the "save" button.

If you want your links to be clickable, copy and paste in the entire address to your profile on another site. For some services this isn't possible, so you can just put in your username or other identifier and we'll display it.
